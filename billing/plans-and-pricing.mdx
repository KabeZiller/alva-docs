## Multi-currency support

Alva supports billing in three currencies:

- **USD** — US Dollars
- **GBP** — British Pounds
- **EUR** — Euros

Your billing currency is set during signup and determines how prices and credits are displayed throughout the app.

## Changing your plan

Go to **Settings → Billing** and click **Change Plan** to upgrade or downgrade. Changes take effect immediately:

- **Upgrading** — you get the new plan's features and credit allocation straight away. You're charged the pro-rated difference.
- **Downgrading** — the new plan takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

## Customer portal

Click **Manage Subscription** in billing settings to open the customer portal where you can:

- Update your payment method
- View invoices and payment history
- Cancel your subscription
