title: "Tags"

---

Tags let you label and categorise companies in your CRM. Use them to mark sources, industries, priorities, or any other grouping that helps you organise your pipeline.

## Managing tags

Go to **Settings → Tags** to create and manage your tags.

Each tag has:

- **Name** — a short label (e.g. "Enterprise", "Inbound Lead", "Trade Show 2026")
- **Colour** — a colour for visual identification

## Creating tags

Click **Add Tag**, enter a name and choose a colour. Tags are available immediately across the platform.

You can also create tags on the fly during [CSV import](/crm/importing-data) — if you need a new tag while importing, you can create it inline without leaving the wizard.

## Applying tags to companies

Tags are applied to companies from:

- The company detail page
- The CSV import wizard (applied to all imported records in a batch)

A company can have multiple tags.

## Filtering by tags

On the Companies page, you can filter the list by tag to quickly find companies with a specific label.

<Tip>
  Set up your tags before you start adding companies and contacts — it's much easier to apply them consistently from the start than to go back and tag everything later.
</Tip>
