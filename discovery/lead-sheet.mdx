The Lead Sheet is where discovered companies become real sales opportunities. It shows every company at the **Lead** lifecycle stage, prioritised by urgency so you know exactly what to act on first.

## Accessing the Lead Sheet

Go to **Discovery → Lead Sheet** in the sidebar.

## How companies get here

Companies appear on the Lead Sheet when they reach **Lead** stage, which happens when:

- A team member manually moves them to Lead from Discovery Companies
- A BDR agent's outreach results in engagement and the company is promoted

## The stats overview

At the top, you'll see five key metrics:

| Metric | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| **Total** | Total companies at Lead stage |
| **Action required** | Companies needing your immediate attention |
| **Drafts to review** | Email replies drafted by a BDR agent, waiting for your approval |
| **Escalated** | Companies flagged by an agent for human review |
| **Awaiting reply** | Companies where outreach was sent but no response yet |

## Priority alerts

Each lead is assigned a priority based on its current state:

- **Action required** — something needs your attention now (a draft to review, an escalation to handle)
- **Awaiting reply** — outreach sent, waiting for the prospect to respond
- **Dormant** — no recent activity, may need re-engagement

Leads requiring action appear at the top of the list.

## Handling drafts

When a BDR agent receives a response from a prospect and has the Draft Reply tool enabled, it writes a suggested response for your review. These appear under "Drafts to review":

- **Approve and send** — send the draft as written
- **Edit and send** — modify the draft before sending
- **Discard** — reject the draft and handle it yourself

## Handling escalations

When an agent escalates a company, you'll see the reason — perhaps the prospect asked a complex question, or the situation needs human judgement. Review the context and decide how to proceed.

## Taking leads further

From the Lead Sheet, your next steps might be:

- **Reply directly** from your email
- **Create a deal** in the CRM for the company
- **Assign to a team member** for follow-up
- **Import into CRM** if not already imported

<Tip>
  Check the Lead Sheet daily. Drafts and escalations are time-sensitive — the faster you respond, the better your conversion rate from lead to deal.
</Tip>
