title: "Phone Numbers"
description: "Get your own calling numbers, set your caller ID, and forward inbound calls."
---

Before you can make calls, your account needs at least one phone number. Numbers are provisioned directly inside Alva — there's no separate phone system to set up — and each number doubles as a caller ID for outbound calls and a line that prospects can call back on.

## Enabling calling

An admin enables calling once for the whole account from **Settings → Telephony**. Click **Enable Telephony** and Alva sets up everything needed behind the scenes, including a default set of [call outcomes](/calling/call-outcomes-and-insights) and your first calling settings.

<Note>
  Only admins can enable calling, add or release numbers, and change call settings. Once calling is enabled, everyone on the team can use the dialler.
</Note>

## Adding a number

Go to **Settings → Telephony → Phone Numbers** and click **Add Number**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a country">
    Select the country you want a number in. Alva shows the countries available to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set an optional area code">
    Enter an area code (for example `020` for London) if you want a number from a specific locality. Leave it blank to see numbers from anywhere in the country.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search and pick a number">
    Alva lists available numbers with their locality and region. Choose the one you want and click **Provision**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your first number is automatically set as the **default** — the caller ID used for outbound calls unless you pick a different one. You can add as many numbers as you need; when you make a call you choose which number to call **from**.

<Tip>
  A local area code lifts answer rates — prospects are far more likely to pick up a number that looks local to them. If you call several regions, provision a number for each.
</Tip>

## Managing your numbers

Each number in the list shows its full number, a friendly name, and a **Default** badge where applicable. From here you can:

- **Forward incoming calls** — see below
- **Release** a number you no longer need

<Warning>
  Releasing a number is permanent. Once released, the number is gone and cannot be recovered — you'd need to provision a new one, which may not be the same number.
</Warning>

## Forwarding inbound calls

When a prospect calls one of your Alva numbers back, you can have that call ring straight through to your mobile or office line.

On each number, set **Forward incoming calls to** with the destination number in full international format (for example `+447812345678`). Inbound calls are then forwarded silently — the caller simply hears it ringing — and your Alva number is shown as the caller ID for the best chance of connecting. Leave the field blank to switch forwarding off.

<Note>
  Forwarding sends the call to your chosen line so you never miss a callback. Inbound calls are not recorded, transcribed, or added to the call log — only the outbound calls you place through Alva are.
</Note>

## Billing

Each active number is charged as a small monthly add-on on your subscription, alongside the per-minute cost of the calls themselves. Releasing a number removes its add-on from your next bill. You can see calling costs broken down in **Settings → Usage**.
