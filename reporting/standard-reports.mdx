Alva includes a set of standard reports that cover the most common sales metrics. These are ready to use out of the box — no setup needed.

## Accessing reports

Go to **Reports** in the sidebar. You'll see your saved reports and can create new ones.

## Available report types

### Pipeline report

Shows the current state of your pipeline:

- Deals by stage with total values
- Average deal size per stage
- Deal count and total value
- Stage conversion rates

### Win/loss report

Analyses your closed deals:

- Win rate over a selected time period
- Win/loss breakdown by reason
- Win/loss by competitor
- Average deal cycle length for won vs lost deals

### Velocity report

Measures how quickly deals move through your pipeline:

- Average time in each stage
- Stage-to-stage conversion rates
- Stalled deals (in the same stage too long)
- Comparison to previous periods

### Methodology adherence

Shows how well your team is following your chosen sales methodology:

- Average completion percentage across all deals
- Per-criterion fill rates (e.g. which MEDDPICC fields are most often left empty)
- Completion by team member
- Completion by deal stage

## Filtering reports

All reports support filters:

- **Date range** — focus on a specific period
- **Deal owner** — filter to a specific team member
- **Stage** — filter by pipeline stage
- **Methodology** — filter by MEDDPICC, BANT, or SPICED

## Exporting

Click **Export CSV** on any report to download the data as a spreadsheet. This is useful for further analysis or sharing with stakeholders who don't have Alva access.
