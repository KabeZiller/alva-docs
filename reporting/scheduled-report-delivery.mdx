---
title: "Scheduled Report Delivery"
description: "Set up reports to be delivered to your inbox automatically on a schedule."
---

You can schedule any report — standard or custom — to be delivered to your inbox automatically. This is useful for weekly pipeline reviews, monthly win/loss summaries, or any report you check regularly.

## Setting up a schedule

1. Open the report you want to schedule
2. Click **Schedule Delivery**
3. Configure the schedule:

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Frequency** | Daily, weekly, or monthly |
| **Day of week** | For weekly reports — which day to send |
| **Day of month** | For monthly reports — which date to send |
| **Recipients** | One or more email addresses |

4. Save the schedule

## What gets delivered

The scheduled email contains:

- A summary of the report's key metrics
- The full report data, either inline or as a CSV attachment
- A link to view the report in Alva with the latest data

## Managing schedules

Go to **Reports** and look for the schedule icon next to any report that has delivery configured. Click it to edit the frequency, recipients, or to disable the schedule.

## Who can set up schedules

Any user can schedule reports for their own inbox. Admins and managers can add other team members as recipients.

<Tip>
  A weekly pipeline report delivered every Monday morning is a great way to start the week with a clear picture of your business — without having to remember to check.
</Tip>
