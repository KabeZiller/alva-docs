title: "AI Settings"

---

AI Settings let you control which AI features are active, how often they run, and how much they're allowed to spend. These are account-wide settings managed by admins.

## Accessing AI settings

Go to **Settings → AI Settings**.

## Feature toggles

You can enable or disable individual AI features:

| Feature | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| **Deal health scoring** | Automatically scores every deal from 0–100 |
| **Risk detection** | Flags deals that show risk signals |
| **Notifications** | Sends in-app alerts when risks are detected |
| **Email analysis** | Analyses email conversations for sentiment, signals, and evidence |
| **Background insights** | Generates periodic AI insights about your pipeline |

Turning off a feature stops it from running and consuming credits. Existing data (health scores, insights) remains — it just won't be updated.

## Budget controls

Set limits on how much AI features can spend:

- **Daily budget cap** — maximum credits AI background features can use per day
- **Monthly budget cap** — maximum credits for the billing period

When a budget cap is reached, background AI features pause automatically. Interactive chat with Alva continues to work — budgets only apply to automated background processing.

## Processing frequency

Control how often background features run:

| Setting | Options |
|---------|---------|
| **Health score refresh** | Hourly, every 6 hours, daily, or weekly |
| **Risk detection** | Hourly, daily, or weekly |
| **Email analysis** | As emails arrive, or batched daily |

Less frequent processing uses fewer credits but means data is less current. For most accounts, the default settings strike a good balance.

<Tip>
  If you're on a limited plan, set background features to daily or weekly rather than hourly. This significantly reduces credit usage while still keeping your data reasonably current.
</Tip>
