Alva can read, create, update, and analyse your CRM data across every area of the platform. Here's a full breakdown of everything Alva can do, organised by category.

## Query and search

Alva can find and display data across your entire CRM:

| Capability | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Count records** | "How many open deals do I have?" |
| **Search companies** | "Find companies in the healthcare industry" |
| **Search contacts** | "Who's the main contact at Acme Corp?" |
| **Search deals** | "Show me deals over £50k in negotiation" |
| **Search activities** | "What meetings happened this week?" |
| **List pipeline stages** | "What stages are in my pipeline?" |
| **Full-text search** | "Search everything for 'pricing proposal'" |

Alva can filter by dates, stages, values, owners, and many other criteria. You can also chain filters naturally: "Show me deals in negotiation that are over £100k and closing this quarter."

## Proactive features

Alva doesn't just respond — it also works proactively in the background:

### Growth ideas

For C-suite users and founders, Alva generates **growth ideas** by analysing your business context, strategy documents, pipeline data, and market position. These are shared naturally in conversation — Alva will mention it has ideas when you start a chat.

### Background health scoring

Alva continuously analyses every deal and updates health scores automatically. You never need to ask — the scores are always current on deal cards and detail pages.

### Risk notifications

When Alva detects a risk in your pipeline (a deal going dark, a health score dropping, a prospect going quiet), it sends a notification to your [notification centre](/getting-started/account-setup).

### Capability gap tracking

If you ask Alva to do something it can't yet do, it logs that as a **capability gap**. This helps the product team understand what features to build next. You don't need to do anything — it happens automatically.
