---
title: "Alva vs Alva Pro"
description: "Understand the difference between Alva and Alva Pro and which is right for you."
---

Alva offers two AI models. You can switch between them at any time using the dropdown in the chat panel.

## The two models

| | Alva | Alva Pro |
|---|------|----------|
| **Speed** | Fast | Slower, but more thorough |
| **Reasoning** | Strong for everyday tasks | Deeper reasoning for complex analysis |
| **Context window** | Large | Very large |
| **Credit cost** | Lower | Higher |
| **Best for** | Daily CRM tasks, queries, record creation | Strategic analysis, complex questions, detailed briefings |

## When to use Alva

Use the standard Alva model for everyday interactions:

- Querying your pipeline — "How many deals close this month?"
- Creating and updating records — "Add a contact for Acme Corp"
- Drafting emails — "Draft a follow-up to Sarah"
- Navigation — "Take me to the deals page"
- Quick analytics — "What's my win rate?"

Alva is fast, cost-effective, and handles the vast majority of tasks perfectly well.

## When to use Alva Pro

Switch to Alva Pro for tasks that benefit from deeper thinking:

- **Complex deal analysis** — "Analyse all my at-risk deals and rank them by likelihood of recovery"
- **Strategic questions** — "Based on our pipeline and strategy, where should we focus next quarter?"
- **Detailed briefing generation** — richer, more nuanced deal briefings
- **Multi-step reasoning** — tasks that require synthesising information from many sources
- **Long conversations** — the larger context window means Alva Pro retains more of the conversation history

## Credit impact

Alva Pro uses more credits per message because the underlying model is more powerful. The exact difference depends on the length of your conversation and the complexity of the response, but as a rough guide, Alva Pro costs approximately 3–5x more per interaction than standard Alva.

You can monitor your credit usage at **Settings → Usage**.

## Switching models

Click the model dropdown at the top of the chat panel to switch. You can switch mid-conversation — the new model picks up the existing context and continues where the previous model left off.

<Tip>
  Start conversations with standard Alva. If the response isn't detailed enough or you need deeper analysis, switch to Alva Pro and ask the same question again.
</Tip>
