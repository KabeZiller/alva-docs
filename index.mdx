## What Alva does

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="CRM">
    Track your companies, contacts, deals, and relationships in one place. Alva scores your deals, flags risks, and tells you what needs attention — without you having to ask.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI Assistant">
    Ask Alva anything about your pipeline in plain language. It reads your deals, emails, and activity to give you a clear picture of your business and tell you exactly what to focus on.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Discovery">
    Search for companies that match your ideal customer profile across more than 158,000 locations worldwide. Filter by geography, industry, and size to build targeted prospect lists instantly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="AI BDR Agents">
    Autonomous agents that search for prospects, write personalised emails, and follow up on your behalf — running 24/7 within the budget and hours you set.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Email Intelligence">
    Connect your inbox and let Alva read every conversation. It surfaces commitments, sentiment, and next steps automatically, and keeps your CRM up to date without manual data entry.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reporting">
    Track pipeline performance, team activity, and revenue trends with built-in reports — or build your own. Schedule reports to land in your inbox automatically.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
